Manipulation

The first task focuses on bimanual object manipulation — coordinating two robotic arms to grasp, reorient, and transfer rigid objects. We explore contact-rich strategies that leverage complementary hand roles (one stabilising, one manipulating) for robust pick-and-place.

Task 3

Scooping

The final and most challenging task: bimanual scooping of granular and deformable materials. Both arms must coordinate to form a scoop geometry, penetrate the material, and lift without excessive spillage. This requires adaptive force control and real-time compliance adjustment based on material feedback.
